Overview

NanoSat MO Framework is an on-board software framework for nanosatellites based on CCSDS Mission Operations services.

By introducing the concept of apps in space, one can easily start/stop the software entity and subsequently monitor and control it. The focus of the development is on the business logic rather than the low-level details of the specific platform. Apps can also retrieve data from the platform and command it using a set of well-defined services. Many possibilities for extensions are available due to its modular and flexible design approach which is not limited to the space segment but extends down to ground by providing all the building blocks for a complete and free end-to-end solution.
